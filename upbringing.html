Upbringing and the Cultivation of his Unnatural Talents

Sequestered in a deep, forested holler, the young Vollmar was able to hone his talents, both intellectual and supernatural.

Even as the trees and forest animals whispered their secrets of deep ecology into his young ears, the principalities of the Earth fought bitter battles for the right to share their eldritch magicks with him.

uniting the natural and supernatural within him." (Book of Omar, 27:5)

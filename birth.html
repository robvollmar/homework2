Upon the subject of his mysterious birth

Much has been said and written upon the mysterious circumstances of his birth but few have had the intellectual courage to sort the dross from the ore.

The sources more concerned with history than hagiography generally agree that he was the end product of some generations long hillbilly eugenics program gone terribly wrong.

And, lo, he shall emerge from the hills they call Aux Arcs
and they shall know him not. (Book of Dooter, 11:15)
